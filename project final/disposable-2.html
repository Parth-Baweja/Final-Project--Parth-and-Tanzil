Description

The Kodak Disposable Camera offers the trusted quality of Kodak film in a convenient, single-use package. This camera is preloaded with 35mm film, making it ready to shoot right out of the box. It’s perfect for vacations, events, or any time you want to capture memories without worrying about camera settings. The built-in flash ensures great results in various lighting conditions, and the simple point-and-shoot operation makes it accessible to everyone, regardless of their photography experience.

Technical Specifications

Film Type 35mm
Lens Fixed focus
Shutter System 1/125 sec
Flash Built-in flash
Battery Included, single use
Dimensions 120 mm (L) x 65 mm (W) x 38 mm (H)
